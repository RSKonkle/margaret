Welcome

I am a program builder, strategist, and fund development leader with experience spanning across the nonprofit advocacy space to economic and community based development and systems change.

My career sits at the intersection of advocacy, economic development, and community based problem-solving. This is demonstrated through my work with projects related to housing, legal access, rural equity, business and industrial development, and property management.

Across roles with Equality Michigan, Albion Economic Development Corporation, and Battle Creek Habitat for Humanity, I have led the creation of new programs and coalitions, secured millions in public and philanthropic funding, and coordinated cross-sector partners at the local, regional, and state levels. I am drawn to early-stage and foundation-setting work that requires vision, persistence, and strategic problem-solving to move from aspiration and implementation.
